Elon Musk is a visionary entrepreneur known for founding SpaceX, Tesla, and several other groundbreaking companies. He is dedicated to advancing technology and pushing the boundaries of what is possible, with a particular focus on sustainable energy and space exploration. Born in South Africa, Musk moved to the United States to pursue his dreams of innovation. His relentless drive and ambitious goals have made him one of the most influential figures in technology and business.
